Introduction

Plant Care & Disease ID (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

The App is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Information Collection and Use
1. Photos & Images

The App allows you to take photos using your device camera or select images from your gallery.

These images are used only for on-device plant disease detection using an offline machine learning model.

Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server.

All image processing happens locally on your device.

2. Personal Information

The App does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts.

No account creation or login is required.

3. Usage Data

The App does not track user behavior, analytics, or usage patterns.

No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs are used.

Permissions Used

The App may request the following permissions:

Camera – To capture photos of plant leaves for disease identification.

Read Media / Photos – To select images from your device gallery.

These permissions are used only when you choose to use related features and are never used in the background.

Data Storage

Any optional scan history or saved results are stored locally on your device only.

The App does not use cloud storage or external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services for data collection.

No advertising SDKs or Google Play Services for ML are used.

The machine learning model (TensorFlow Lite) runs entirely offline.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Since no personal data is collected at all, it is safe for general audiences.

Medical & Agricultural Disclaimer

The App provides informational suggestions only based on image recognition.

Results may not always be accurate.

The App is not a substitute for professional agricultural, botanical, or expert advice.

Data Security

Since no personal or sensitive data is collected or transmitted, the risk to user privacy is minimal.

All processing is done locally on the device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected within the App or its Play Store listing.
